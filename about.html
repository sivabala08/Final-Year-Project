Our Mission

Life Connect is dedicated to revolutionizing the blood donation ecosystem in India by connecting donors, recipients, and blood banks through an integrated digital platform. Our mission is to ensure timely access to safe blood for everyone in need while promoting voluntary blood donation as a civic responsibility.

Our Vision

We envision a future where no life is lost due to blood shortage. Life Connect aims to be the backbone of India's blood transfusion services, making the process transparent, efficient, and accessible to all citizens regardless of their geographical location or socioeconomic status.

Our Journey

Launched as a national initiative, Life Connect has grown to become India's largest blood donation network. Starting with a handful of blood banks, we now connect over 3,800 registered blood banks across 29 states and 8 Union Territories. Our platform has facilitated millions of successful donations, saving countless lives and establishing new standards for blood banking in the country.
